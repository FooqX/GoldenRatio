<p><i>Golden Ratio</i> is a FOSS Android calculator application, that helps you to calculate a, b, sum using the <a href='https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_ratio' target='_blank' rel='nofollow noopener'>Golden Ratio</a>.</p><p>This is a special calculator, not just a basic a+b calculator. It calculates based on the input, and fills the blank spaces. It <b>can</b> calculate:</p><ol><li><b>A and B</b> based on the sum value;</li><li><b>A and Result</b> based on B value;</li><li><b>B and Result</b> based on A value;</li></ol><p>So, that means you're only allowed to enter:</p><ul><li>A</li><li>B</li><li>SUM</li></ul><p>and the rest it will automatically calculate using the Golden ratio and basic math (subtraction, addition).</p><p>To clear the entered values after they have been calculated, click the "Calculate" button again. The "Clear" button only clears the history.</p><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>No ads, tracking.</li><li>Free and open source (FOSS)</li><li>Available on IzzyOnDroid</li><li>Fully translated in English, Latvian.</li><li>Shows calculation history</li><li>Calculate a, b, sum easily using Golden Ratio</li><li>Shows which quantities were calculated using the Golden ratio in history (it's denoted by the Ï† symbol)</li><li>Simple interface</li></ul>
