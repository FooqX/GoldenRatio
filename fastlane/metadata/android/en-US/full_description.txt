<p><i>Golden Ratio</i> is a free and open-source (FOSS) Android calculator designed to help you calculate proportional values based on the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Golden_ratio" target="_blank" rel="nofollow noopener">Golden Ratio</a>. Whether you're a designer, mathematician, or just curious about the ancient Greek concept of harmonic proportions, this app makes the process easy and intuitive.</p><p>This isn't just a basic A+B calculator, it intelligently fills in missing values based on the <i>Golden ratio</i>. It can compute:</p><ol><li><b>A and B</b> based on the SUM value;</li><li><b>A and SUM</b> based on B value;</li><li><b>B and SUM</b> based on A value.</li></ol><p><b>How to use:</b></p><ul><li>Enter exactly <b>one</b> value: A, B, or SUM.</li><li>The app will <i>automatically calculate</i> the missing 2 values using the <i>Golden Ratio</i> (and basic math).</li><li>Click "Calculate" to calculate, and <b>click it again to clear the inputs</b>.</li><li>Click "Clear" to reset only the <b>history</b>.</li></ul><p><br><b>Features:</b></p><ul><li>No ads or tracking</li><li>FOSS</li><li>Available on IzzyOnDroid</li><li>Fully translated in English and Latvian.</li><li>Shows calculation history</li><li>Calculate a, b, sum easily using Golden Ratio</li><li>Calculated values based on the Golden Ratio are marked with the Ï† symbol in history</li><li>Simple interface</li><li>Very lightweight</li></ul>
